Nearly Now is a photography zine I made in september 2023. A state I found myself in during the months, as these photos were being taken. It is a feeling of being suspended—neither fully being in the present, nor in the past, nor entirely looking ahead to the future. A balancing act between holding on and letting go. These photographs are my homage to skateboarding, to the warm summers in Landau, and to the friends who made my time. They are fragments of time—snapshots of joy, nostalgia, and transition, that I wanted to hold onto before stepping into a new chapter of life. With Nearly Now, I wanted to capture the fleeting beauty of those final moments, preserving them before moving forward.
